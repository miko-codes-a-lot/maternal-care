Maternal Care Application

Designed to empower prenatal care, the Maternal Care Application integrates advanced technology with healthcare needs. Built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, it bridges the gap between health workers and expectant mothers.

Overview

The Maternal Care Application enhances prenatal care by providing a user-friendly platform for health monitoring and management, ensuring both efficiency and accessibility.

Key Objectives

  • User-Friendly Interface: Simplify access for pregnant women and healthcare professionals.
  • Centralized Data Management: Allow health workers to efficiently manage pregnancy-related information.
  • Timely Notifications: Ensure mothers receive reminders for checkups and vaccinations.
  • Data Analytics: Provide actionable insights to assist health workers in decision-making.
  • Secure Data Storage: Protect patient records with reliable storage and backup solutions.

Core Features

  • Personalized Health Profiles: Offer detailed user profiles containing medical history and schedules.
  • Automated Notifications: Deliver reminders for appointments and important updates.
  • Real-time Updates: Enable health workers to record and track patient conditions instantly.
  • Analytics Tools: Generate comprehensive data for improving health outcomes.
  • Backup and Security: Ensure all health records are safeguarded and recoverable.

User Roles

  • Midwives: Manage health records, monitor pregnancies, and oversee health worker accounts.
  • Barangay Health Workers (BHW): Update patient data, organize schedules, and support communication with patients.
  • Pregnant Women: Access health profiles, receive reminders, and communicate with healthcare providers.

Technologies Used

  • Android Studio: For app development.
  • MongoDB: Database for secure record storage.
  • Adobe XD: For user interface design.
  • Agile Methodology: Ensuring iterative development with user feedback.

Benefits

  • For Midwives: Simplified management of patient records and comprehensive analytics tools.
  • For Health Workers: Real-time updates and improved communication systems.
  • For Pregnant Women: Improved prenatal care through timely notifications and easy access to health data.
