Overview

Welcome to Maternal Care Application. It is a comprehensive digital solution designed to enhance prenatal and maternal care. It provides a user-friendly platform for health monitoring, ensuring efficiency, accessibility, and personalized care. By integrating advanced features, it empowers expectant mothers and healthcare providers to collaborate effectively, fostering better health outcomes. Key functionalities include real-time health tracking, appointment scheduling, and reminders, making it a one-stop platform for maternal well-being.

HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS?

The Maternal Care Application is designed to streamline maternal care and make it easy for healthcare providers to manage patient data efficiently. Below are the main functions of the system:

Pregnant Users

The system collects and stores detailed information about each pregnant user, including personal details, medical history, health records, and pregnancy progress. This data helps in monitoring the health of Pregnant Women.

Messages

BHWs can send direct messages to pregnant users, updating them on appointments, health checks, or providing advice and guidance.

Reminders

BHWs receive automated reminders for upcoming appointments, check-ups, and health monitoring tasks. These notifications help ensure timely care and follow-ups.

Backup Storage

All patient data is securely stored in the cloud with a backup system in place. The system ensures that the records of pregnant women are safely stored for up to 5 years, providing long-term access to health information for future reference or health needs.

Settings

The BHWs can see her profile, preferences, and settings.
