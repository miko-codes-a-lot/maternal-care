Overview

Welcome to Maternal Care Application. It is a comprehensive digital solution designed to enhance prenatal and maternal care. It provides a user-friendly platform for health monitoring, ensuring efficiency, accessibility, and personalized care. By integrating advanced features, it empowers expectant mothers and healthcare providers to collaborate effectively, fostering better health outcomes. Key functionalities include real-time health tracking, appointment scheduling, and reminders, making it a one-stop platform for maternal well-being.

HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS?

The Maternal Care Application is designed to streamline maternal care and make it easy for healthcare providers to manage patient data efficiently. Below are the main functions of the system:

Profile

Each pregnant woman has her own profile where she can view important health information, such as medical history, pregnancy progress, and upcoming appointments. This allows her to stay informed about her health status and track key milestones during her pregnancy.

Messages

The app enables pregnant women to send messages to their assigned Barangay Health Worker (BHW) for any questions, concerns, or updates about their health. This feature ensures direct and secure communication between the patient and the healthcare provider.

Reminder

The app sends automatic reminders to pregnant women about upcoming check-ups, appointments, or important health tasks. These reminders help ensure they don’t miss any critical appointments or health checks, improving the overall care process.

Settings

In the settings section, pregnant women can view and manage their personal information and account details. This includes updating contact information, adjusting notification preferences, and ensuring the accuracy of their health records.

The Maternal Care Application is a powerful tool designed to enhance maternal healthcare by improving communication, organization, and efficiency for all users—expectant mothers, midwives, and Barangay Health Workers (BHWs). It simplifies the monitoring of pregnancies, provides easy access to critical health information, and ensures timely interventions through features like secure messaging, reminders, and health tracking. For BHWs, it enhances their ability to track and support pregnant women in their communities, while ensuring they have quick access to the data and resources they need. With a focus on efficiency, security, and accessibility, the app empowers healthcare providers to deliver higher-quality care and ensures that no mother or baby is overlooked. The system ultimately leads to better health outcomes by improving both individual and community-based healthcare management, making it a valuable resource in maternal health services.
